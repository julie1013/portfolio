Simpsons and Chill!

I am proud to announce my very first Ember project, Simpsons and Chill! This app allows the user to add their favorite Simpsons episodes to and remove them from a personal playlist. Learning Ember was really challenging, yet rewarding-- I realize that there is a lot more to learn about it and I look forward to doing it.

A work in progress, the sky's the limit for Simpsons and Chill!

This Book Sucks!

What if you would rather read "bad" books than "good" books?

My first full-stack app and my first foray into Rails, this work-in-progress allows users to add books to and remove them from a to-read list. Eventually, it will allow users to find reasons to remove books from their elephantine to-read lists via scathing reviews from other people.
